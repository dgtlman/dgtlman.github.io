About Eric Garofalo

Eric Garofalo has been working in the computer industry for over 25 years. It all got started when he was 21 and his grandmother lent him the money to buy his first computer. Since then it was something that has come naturally to him.

Things really got started when he got Microsoft Certified for Windows 2000 (MCSE) and then worked for a full solution company in Petersburg, VA, as the Lead IT Specialist. He learned how to run an ISP, do in shop and onsite service calls, and manage phone tech support.

Since then, Eric has been been increasing his computer abilities and running his own company focused on providing a full set of solutions for business and home customers. Eric has focused on providing high quality work at reasonable service rates.
