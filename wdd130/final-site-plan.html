OVERVIEW

Purpose:

This site will be to market my professional skills to clients. This will allow me to make money while in school by applying principles and concepts I am learning. It will also provide experience to make it easier to apply for jobs after I graduate; or build a successful company.

Audience:

The site will address what services and experience that I have and how to contact me. Largely, these service will be based on a 1:1 need and customized and catered to individual/company needs. While there are MANY other sites that are similar in content and services to what I will be offering, because the services I will offer are largely location based, I would imagine that about 90% of the target market will be geographically close to me. As services and skillset increase, I would hope to expand these services beyond my local area.
