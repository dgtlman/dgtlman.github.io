Target Audience of GEMi:

  • Who: all local residents, Eastern Idaho and people visiting the area.
  • Age: 12 and up (male or female)
  • Income: Site services will appeal to local community independant of income.

Scenerios:

  • There is an outdoor event this weekend. How should I dress based on the weather?
  • Looking for a place to eat out, what are some local restaurants?
  • What are the upcoming local events?
  • What are the current local fishing conditions?
  • Will this be a good weekend to go cycling?

Persona 1 - Jake Sully: Married, 33.

Jake is a former, marine who was injured in the line of duty. He is now working for the family construction business. He has been married to his high school sweetheart for 12 years and they have 3 kids. They live a comfortable modest lifestyle and are always looking for local activies to do as a family. He also enjoys finishing with his older two children. Jake will use the site to see what local events are going on, fishing conditions, and how weather will affect activities. His prefered device is his mobile phone.

Grace spent many years teaching at Boston University. She and her husband were very career focused and only have one daughter. After her husband passed away, Grace needed a change of pace.
