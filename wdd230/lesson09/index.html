Winter is Here : Top 10 things to winterize

  • Check the gutters: Clear gutters of debris
  • Protect Pipes: Insulate at risk pipes
  • Seal the cracks: Install Weather Striping and seal holes/cracks.
  • Stop the slips: Divert drainage away from walkways driveways. Install handrails.
  • Install an emergency release : Consider installing an emergency release to prevent water pipes bursting
  • Keep Cozy : Make sure your house is well insulated and set your thermostat to 65°
  • Have your furnace checked: Service your heating and cooling atleast once a year...especially before you need them the most.
  • Test your Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Detectors: They should be tested monthly and batteries replaced often.
  • Trim trees and clear yard: Snow is heavy on branches, prevent damage to your home and others by removing dead or weak branches.
  • Remove Hoses: Drain and store hoses inside to prevent pipes bursting and prolong the life of your hoses. Insulate outdoor faucets.
