Domain Name:

The site name is based on Idaho's state nickname (the Gem state) and Greater Eastern Metropolitan Idaho (GEMi). The site will be stylized as GEMi.link and cost $13 per year.

Site Purpose:

GEMi.link will be landing site to focus on local information. It will also provide weather conditions, outdoor events, community information and resouces related to the area.
